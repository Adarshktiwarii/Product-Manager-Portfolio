Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

This website (adarshktiwari.co.in) is operated by Adarsh Tiwari, an independent technology consultant based in Bangalore, India. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Collected

The following data may be collected when you use this website:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and message content — submitted voluntarily through the consultation form.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address — submitted voluntarily through the newsletter form.
  • Analytics data: Page views, scroll depth, click interactions, browser type, device type, approximate location (via timezone), and referral source — collected automatically via Mixpanel.
  • Local storage: A randomly generated visitor ID and analytics event data stored in your browser's localStorage.

How Data Is Used

  • To respond to consultation inquiries and project requests
  • To send newsletter updates (only if you subscribe)
  • To understand how visitors use the website and improve the experience
  • To measure the effectiveness of content and services

Third-Party Services

  • Formspree: Processes contact form and newsletter submissions. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel: Provides website analytics. Mixpanel Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Loads typefaces. Google Privacy Policy
  • Font Awesome (Cloudflare CDN): Loads icons.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained as long as needed to respond and follow up. Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data in localStorage is automatically cleaned after 30 days or 500 records.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to any personal data held about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time
  • Clear localStorage in your browser to remove analytics data

To exercise any of these rights, email consult@adarshktiwari.co.in.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies directly. Mixpanel and other third-party services may set their own cookies. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact consult@adarshktiwari.co.in.
